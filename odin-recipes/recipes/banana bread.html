Banana Bread

Description

This recipe is a classic that I've been making pretty regularly lately. the perfect softness
combined with a delicious banana flavor. Who doesn't like a good banana bread?

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter(softened)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup bananas
  • 2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350oF
  2. In a bowl whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  3. In a second bowl mix the sugar and butter until fluffy (about 2min)
  4. Add in the eggs one at a time
  5. add in the mashed bananas, lemon juice, and vanilla extract
  6. Mix well, it chould look a little curdled at this point
  7. Add in the dry Ingredients
  8. Mix in until incorporated
  9. Pour the batter into a pan and bake for around 45 minutes
  10. Let cool for 10 min
