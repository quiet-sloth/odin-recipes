Menudo

Description

This is my dads favorite winter breakfast. It's also super simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs. raw tripe
  • 24oz can of hominy
  • jalapeno
  • onion
  • lime juice
  • 1 pckg of menudo mix

Steps

  1. Cut the tripe into 2in. squares
  2. Boil the tripe for one hour.
  3. Add the Menudo mix and hominy
  4. Bring to a simmer for another hour.
  5. It should be ready to bowl.
  6. Add diced jalapeno, onion, and lime juice as needed.
