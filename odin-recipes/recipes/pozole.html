Pozole

Description

This is a recipe I love to do during winter for a delicious breakfast.
It's simple, easy, and can be left to simmer all night!

Ingredients

  • Menudo spice mix
  • 2lbs of raw pork
  • 24oz can of homony
  • 1 Large jalapeno
  • 1 medium onion
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Cut pork into 1in. cubes
  2. Boil pork till pork is tender
  3. Add in menudo mix and homony
  4. Let simmer for 30 minutes
  5. It's ready for the bowl
  6. Add diced jalapeno, onion, and lime juice to liking
